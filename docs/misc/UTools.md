# UTools

**Developer:** Angelus

uTools is an add-on for [Mozilla Firefox](Mozilla_Firefox.md) especially created for [Utopia](Utopia.md). It formats the Utopia pages and improves the general interface.

Download: http://www.k3ltic.com/utools (compatible with new code)

## Requirements

- Mozilla Firefox

## Features

- Redo spells and ops multiple times on the same province without re-selecting anything
- Kingdom pages may be sorted by all fields
- Throne page formatted by TPA, WPA, OPA, DPA, pPA
- BPA on Science page
- Bookmark & comment on kingdoms for easy selection on Mystics, Thievery, War Room & Kingdom pages
- Mass messaging
- War Room calculator
- Ambush calculator
- Inbuilt Forum Agent (supports multiple agents)
- Generate SOT/SOM/SURVEY/SOS
- Foreman (stores and populates your build/strat)
- External kingdom paper formatter
