# Metatron Plus

Also known as **Metatron**.

**Developer:** celien

Metatron Plus is an add-on for [Mozilla Firefox](Mozilla_Firefox.md) especially created for [Utopia](Utopia.md). It formats the Utopia pages and improves the general interface.

> **Note:** No longer supported on new code. The Metatron team recommends [UTools](UTools.md) instead, a similar add-on that is more actively developed.

Download: https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/9653

## Requirements

- Mozilla Firefox

## Features

- Redo spells and ops multiple times on the same province without re-selecting anything
- Kingdom pages sortable by province name, networth, land, NWPA or honor
- Throne page formatted by TPA, WPA, OPA, DPA
- All locations are clickable and link to their kingdom page
- Bookmark & comment on kingdoms for easy selection on Mystics, Thievery, War Room, Message & Kingdom pages
- Mass message your kingdom
- War Room calculator
- Summarizes kingdom pages
