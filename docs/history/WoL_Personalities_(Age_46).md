# WoL Personalities (Age 46)

This is a list of the current bonuses and penalties for the Utopian Personalities on the [WoL](World_of_Legends.md) server for Age 46

For previous bonuses, see [WoL Personalities (Age 45)](WoL_Personalities_(Age_45).md)

### The Merchant

Ruler Title: The Wealthy

- +25% Income
- -25% Money Losses on Plunder Attacks
- Immunity to all Income Penalties (Riots, Plagues, etc.)

### The Shepherd

Ruler Title: The Humble

- Land generates 4 bushels of food per acre daily
- Immunity to food penalties from Drought & Storms
- Immune to Plague
- -30% Explore time
- -30% Explore costs (Soldiers & Gold):
- +10% Population Limit
- +15% Birth Rates

### The Sage

Ruler Title: The Wise

- +30% Science Effectiveness
- -30% Book Costs
- -30% Learn Time
- -50% Losses on Learn Attacks
- Every 2 Acres of land produces 1 Science Book per Hour

### The Rogue

Ruler Title: The Rogue

- +1 Stealth recovery per tick
- Access to all thievery operations, including 3 unique to rogues:

- Greater Arson
- Assassinate Wizards
- Propaganda

### The Mystic

Ruler Title: The Sorcerer

- Meteor Shower spell
- All Guilds are twice as effective

### The Warrior

Ruler Title: The Warrior

- -50% Land Losses when Ambushed
- +15% Enemy Casualties (on attack or defense)
- -15% Your Military Casualties (on attack or defense)
- -15% Attack Time

### The Tactician

Ruler Title: The Conniving

- Access to enhanced Conquest Attack at all times
- Gain 50% more Specialist and Building Credits
- -30% Military training time
- -30% Military Wages
- -30% Draft Cost

### The Artisan

Ruler Title: The Craftsman

- -30% Building Construction Time
- +20% Building Efficiency
- +50% Protection of Buildings from Tornadoes, Arson Operations, Raze and Massacre Attacks
- All Capacity and Flat Rate Buildings are 25% more Effective
