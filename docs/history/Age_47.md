# Age 47 — The Age of Justice

- **Forum Source:** [Age 47 Changes (confirmed)](https://forums.utopia-game.com/showthread.php?610522)

## Overview

This forum source is a brief confirmed preview rather than a full age changelog.

- The most significant changes for the new age will be in Thievery.
- Halflings will be returning to Utopia as a strong thief race.
- Thievery operations will be split into two categories: Espionage and Sabotage.
- Espionage will consist of information-gathering operations, while Sabotage will involve destructive operations.
- Crystal Ball and Crystal Eye will be removed from spells and added as espionage thievery operations.
- The final names for those operations were still under discussion in the preview thread.
- The intent was to keep those operations from becoming significantly harder to obtain successfully than before.
- Additional bonus and race-mod changes were mentioned, but not detailed in this preview.
