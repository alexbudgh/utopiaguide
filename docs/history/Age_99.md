# Age 99 — Crossing Boundaries

- **WoL Age 99 Start:** Monday, November 21, 2022 @ 18:00 GMT
- **WoL Age 99 End:** Saturday, February 4, 2023 @ 00:00 GMT
- **Duration:** 76
- **Forum Source:** [Age 99 Final Changes](https://forums.utopia-game.com/showthread.php?640313-Age-99-Final-Changes)

### Mechanical Changes

- Ambush Attack inflicts 15% increased Military Kills.
- Peasant kills on Massacre Attack reduced ~30%.
- The losing Kingdom of a War receives Science Books equal to 24 Ticks of production.
- The losing Kingdom of a War does not transfer (lose) Honor to the winner.
- Out of Range Wars will not award War Score.
- Players can cancel their mentor.

### [Spells](../guide/Mystics.md)

- Meteor Showers - availability changed to Hostile/War.
- Mind Focus - bonus effect increased to 35% (up from 25%).
- Mystic Vortex - cost will be reduced when cast on Kingdom mates and a bot request will be available.
