# WoL Races (Age 46)

This is a list of the current benefits and limitations for the Utopian Races on the WoL server for Age 46

For previous bonuses, see [WoL Races (Age 45)](WoL_Races_(Age_45).md)

## Humans

Benefits

- +15% Income
- +15% Thievery Effectiveness
- Defensive Specialist Strength +1

Limitations

- Offensive Specialist Strength -1
- -25% Chance of Spell Success

Specific Spellbook

- Greater Protection
- Aggression

Units

- Offensive Specialist – Swordsman
- Defensive Specialist – Archer
- Elite Unit – Knight (8/3, $800, 6.5 nw)

## Elves

Benefits

- +30% Chance of Spell Success
- +1 Mana Recovery per Tick

Limitations

- No Access to Dungeons
- -15% Gains from Thief Ops

Specific Spellbook

- Mystic Aura
- Clear Sight
- Fountain of Knowledge
- Pitfalls
- Amnesia
- Quick Feet
- Reflect Magic

Units

- Offensive Specialist – Ranger
- Defensive Specialist – Archer
- Elite Unit – Elf Lord (7/4, $800, 6.0 nw)

## Dwarves

Benefits

- +30% Building Efficiency
- Free Building Construction
- Offensive Specialist Strength +1
- Immunity to Fog

Limitations

- Can't use Accelerated Construction

Specific Spellbook

- Fog
- Animate Dead

Units

- Offensive Specialist – Warrior
- Defensive Specialist – Axeman
- Elite Unit – Berserker (7/4, $800, 6.0 nw)

## Orcs

Benefits

- +30% Gains on Attacks
- Spreads and is immune to the Plague
- Train Elites with Specialist Credits

Limitations

- Defensive Specialist Strength -1
- -50% Honor

Specific Spellbook

- Fanaticism
- Aggression

Units

- Offensive Specialist – Goblin
- Defensive Specialist – Troll
- Elite Unit – Ogre (9/2, $850, 6.75 nw)

## Gnomes

Benefits

- +40% Thievery Effectiveness
- -50% Cost of Training Thieves
- +1 Stealth Recovery per Tick

Limitations

- No Access to Hospitals

Specific Spellbook

- Quick Feet
- Town Watch
- Vermin
- War Spoils

Units

- Offensive Specialist – Halfling
- Defensive Specialist – Pikeman
- Elite Unit – Golem (5/5, $400, 4.0 nw)

## Dark Elves

Benefits

- +30% Spell Duration
- +30% Spell Efficiency
- Spells do not Consume Runes

Limitations

- none

Specific Spellbook

- Invisibility
- Fool’s Gold
- Nightmares
- Tree of Gold

Units

- Offensive Specialist – Night Ranger
- Defensive Specialist – Druid
- Elite Unit – Drow (6/4, $650, 5.0 nw)

## Avians

Benefits

- -25% Attack Time
- +15% Gains from Thief Ops
- Immunity to Ambush

Limitations

- No Access to Stables

Specific Spellbook

- Clearsight
- Townwatch

Units

- Offensive Specialist – Griffin
- Defensive Specialist – Harpie
- Elite Unit - Drake (8/3, $800 6.5 nw)
