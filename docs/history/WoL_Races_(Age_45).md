# WoL Races (Age 45)

This is a list of the current benefits and limitations for the Utopian Races on the WoL server for [Age](#) 45

## Humans

Benefits

- Income : +25%
- Science Effectiveness : +30%
- Defensive Specialist Strength : +1

Limitations

- Magic Effectiveness : -25%

Specific Spellbook

- Greater Protection
- Aggression

Units

- Offensive Specialist – Swordsman
- Defensive Specialist – Archer
- Elite Unit – Knight (6/3, $750, 5.25 nw)

## Elves

Benefits

- Magic Effectiveness : +30%
- Mana Recovery / Hour : +1
- Combat Losses : -50%
- Wages: -50%

Limitations

- No Access to Dungeons

Specific Spellbook

- Mystic Aura
- Clear Sight
- Fountain of Knowledge
- Pitfalls
- Amnesia
- Quick Feet
- Reflect Magic

Units

- Offensive Specialist – Ranger
- Defensive Specialist – Archer
- Elite Unit – Elf Lord (6/4, $700, 5.5 nw)

## Dwarves

Benefits

- Building Efficiency : +30%
- Free Building Construction
- Immunity to Fog

Limitations

- Thievery Effectiveness : -30%
- Can't use Accelerated Construction

Specific Spellbook

- Fog
- Animate Dead

Units

- Offensive Specialist – Warrior
- Defensive Specialist – Axeman
- Elite Unit – Berserker (7/4, $1000, 6.75 nw)

## Orcs

Benefits

- Gains on Attacks : +30%
- Draft Costs : -50%

Limitations

Specific Spellbook

- Fanaticism
- Aggression

Units

- Offensive Specialist – Goblin
- Defensive Specialist – Troll
- Elite Unit – Ogre (8/2, $800, 6.75 nw)
