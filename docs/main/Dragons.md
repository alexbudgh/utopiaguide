# Dragons

Dragons are sent by a kingdom's Monarch or Steward to disrupt an opposing kingdom. Provinces donate gold to fund the dragon. Once sent, a dragon wreaks havoc for **five full months (120 ticks)** or until slain.

*Last updated: Age 115*

## General Mechanics

- Troops slay a dragon based on their offensive value. A soldier with 3/0 deals 3 damage per unit; an elite with 14/4 deals **14** damage (the higher of the two values — offence and defence are no longer combined).
- Thieves and Wizards **cannot** slay dragons.
- Dragon HP has been reduced by **12%** compared to pre-Age 114 levels (−10% in Age 114, −2% in Age 115).
