    Age 99 — Crossing Boundaries

    • WoL Age 98 End: Saturday, November 19, 2022 @ 00:00 GMT
    • WoL Age 99 Open: Saturday, November 19, 2022 @ 12:00 GMT
    • WoL Age 99 Start: Monday, November 21, 2022 @ 18:00 GMT
    • WoL Age 99 End: Saturday, February 4, 2023 @ 00:00 GMT
    • Duration: 76
    • Forum Source: Age 99 Final Changes

    General Mechanics

    • Ambush Attack inflicts 15% increased Military Kills
    • Peasant kills on Massacre Attack reduced by about 30%
    • The losing Kingdom of a War receives Science Books equal to 24 ticks of production
    • The losing Kingdom of a War does not transfer Honor to the winner
    • Out-of-range Wars do not award War Score
    • Players can cancel their mentor

    Spells

    • Meteor Showers availability changed to Hostile/War
    • Mind Focus bonus effect increased to 35%, up from 25%
    • Mystic Vortex cost is reduced when cast on Kingdom mates and a bot request is available
