    Changes from Age 115

    For detailed diffs, see Race Comparison, Personality Comparison, and Dragons & Rituals Comparison.

    Core Mechanics

    • War Doctrine per-province increment reduced to +1% per additional province (was +1.5%), max still 12.5%; Elf, Faery, and Halfling keep +2% per additional province
    • Unfriendly/Hostile relations (15/30 meter points) now unlock matching Unfriendly spells and thievery operations against that kingdom
    • Fog of War removed from OOP
    • Watch Towers catch chance: 2.0% → 2.2%
    • Shielding and Valor science multipliers increased; Cunning and Sorcery merged into a single tree, Arcana
    • Several spell/thievery tweaks (Fireball, Storms, Droughts, Chastity, Sloth, Magic Ward, Lightning Strike removed from global access; Incite Riots, Bribe Thieves, Free Prisoners, Rob the Vaults, Kidnap, Destabilise Guilds, Arson)
    • War Learn/Plunder attacks now also capture land; Massacre peasant damage in War +5%
    • Out of War attack gain penalty raised from 10% to 15%
    • Hostile Meter decay reverts to pre-Age 111 rules and no longer resets on Ceasefire or entering War
    • DFCF replaced by FCF with new thresholds and reduced penalties
    • No changes to Rituals were announced this age

    Dragons

    • Amethyst, Emerald, and Ruby arrival/tick damage increased
    • Topaz Building Efficiency penalty reduced (−30% → −25%)
    • Sapphire rune-destruction tick rate doubled (every 6 → every 3 ticks) and arrival damage increased
    • Per-dragon Cost Modifiers replaced by a flat +15% Gold and Food cost for dragons started outside War

    Races

    • New Race: Dryad
    • Avian: unique replaced (Aerial Supremacy); BE penalty replaced with +30% Rune Cost; elite 16/2
    • Dark Elf: gains +30% Offensive WPA, loses Rune Cost bonus; unique replaced (Dark Ritual); reworked spell book and units
    • Dwarf: gains −50% Construction Cost; unique replaced (Mountain Hold); Attack Time penalty replaced with +90% Food Consumption
    • Elf: Rune Production bonus replaced with +30% Offensive Spell Duration; unique replaced (Arcane Mastery); TPA penalty replaced with +35% Draft Costs
    • Faery: WPA/TPA and Self Spell Duration increased; explicit spell book replaces "All Racial Spells"; new +15% Military Casualties penalty
    • Halfling: Population and TPA bonuses increased; unique replaced (Burrowed Networks); Military Casualties penalty replaced with −25% Draft Speed
    • Human: fully reworked — income/training/draft-speed bonuses replace prisoner/science bonuses; unique replaced (Muster the Realm); penalties simplified to +25% Military Wages
    • Orc: gains +15% Enemy Military Casualties; unique replaced (Blood Spoils); DME penalty replaced with +20% Instant Damage taken
    • Undead: Death March nerfed (45% → 25%); spell book updated; Science Efficiency penalty replaced with Cannot Build Hospitals

    Personalities

    • Paladin removed; Cleric reintroduced; new Personality: Sage
    • Artisan: Building Capacity/Production reduced; gains −25% Construction Costs; unique replaced (Master Craftsmanship)
    • General: gains +2 Offensive Elite Strength; Training Cost/Speed increased to −25%; unique replaced (Relentless Assault)
    • Heretic: TPA/WPA raised to +35%; Guild Effectiveness reduced to +50%; unique replaced (Arcane Frenzy)
    • Mystic: Guild Effectiveness reduced to +100%; Spell Duration bonus replaced with +25% WPA; unique threshold raised to 60% mana
    • Necromancer: WPA and Rune Cost bonuses reduced; ME and Channeling Efficiency increased; Dark Pact conversion rates reduced
    • Rogue: TPA and Crime Science Efficiency increased
    • Tactician: Attack Time reduced further; Draft Speed increased; Ambush Losses bonus removed; Interdiction now a flat 15%/15%/15%
    • War Hero: gains Battle Gains and Honor Loss bonuses; Honor Effects raised to +100%; unique replaced (Rising Renown)
    • Warrior: Tactics Science Efficiency increased; Merc Cost bonus removed; Battle Cry damage increased to 1.5%

    Core Mechanic Changes

    War Doctrine

    • 1st Province: +2.0%
    • Each Additional Province: +1%
    • Maximum: 12.5%

    Elf, Faery, and Halfling War Doctrines gain +2% strength from each additional province after the first, instead of +1%.

    Relations & Hostility

    When a kingdom's attitude becomes Unfriendly/Hostile (15/30 meter points) towards another kingdom, Unfriendly Spells and Operations become available to use against that kingdom:

    • Spells: Fireball, Lightning Strike, Fools Gold, Meteor Showers
    • Thievery: Nightstrike, Greater Arson

    If two kingdoms' attitudes are Unfriendly/Hostile towards each other, both gain these Unfriendly Spells and Operations against each other.

    Fog of War removed from OOP.

    Out of War Attack Penalty: the Gain penalty is lifted from 10% to 15%.

    Hostile Meter

    • Meter decay reverts to the rules used before Age 111.
    • Hostile Meter points decay on the 1st of each month by either 20% or 3 points, whichever is greater.
    • Hostile Meter no longer resets for Ceasefires or Entering Wars.

    FCF

    FCF becomes available for a defender when all of the following are met:

    • The defender is 30 points (in-range) or 15 points (out-of-range) below the aggressor on the hostility meter
    • The defender is not Hostile towards the aggressor
    • No attacks have been made against the aggressor for 3 ticks
    • The defender is lower in both Land and Networth than the aggressor

    If within War Range of the aggressor (15% NW or Land), FCF incurs a penalty of 2% Honor and 2% Science loss; there is no penalty if out of War Range. Duration is 96 hours if out of range, and 72 hours if in range, before either kingdom can cancel it.

    Buildings

    Watch Towers: catch chance increased from 2.0% to 2.2%.

    Science

    • Shielding multiplier increased from 0.0314 to 0.0350
    • Valor multiplier increased from 0.0582 to 0.0620
    • Cunning and Sorcery are merged back into one tree for instant spell and thief damage, known as Arcana

    Spells

    • Fireball peasant damage increased from 4–7% to 5–7%
    • Storms peasant losses per tick increased from 1.5% to 1.75%
    • Droughts food reduction increased from 25% to 30%
    • Droughts draft rate reduction increased from 15% to 20%
    • Chastity base duration average reduced from 6 to 4 days
    • Sloth base duration average reduced from 6 to 4 days
    • Magic Ward base duration average reduced from 6 to 4 days
    • Lightning Strike removed from global access

    Thievery

    • Incite Riots income reduction increased from 15% to 20%
    • Bribe Thieves TPA reduction increased from 10% to 12.5%
    • Free Prisoners damage increased from 17% to 25%
    • Rob the Vaults War Cap lifted to 16%
    • Kidnap War Cap lifted to 5%
    • Destabilise Guilds spell duration reduction increased from 20% to 25%
    • Arson damage increased by 5%

    Attacking and Defending

    • In War, successful Learn and Plunder attacks also capture land equal to 20% of the acres a Traditional March would have gained.
    • Massacre Peasant Damage in War: +5% (Additive). Thief and Wizard damage unchanged.

    Dragons

    Dragons started outside War cost 15% more Gold and Food.

    Amethyst Dragon

    • −40% Spell Success Chance
    • −40% Thievery Success Chance on sabotage operations
    • All active self-spells are removed instantly upon arrival, and every 6 ticks thereafter, kills 5% of wizards and thieves

    Emerald Dragon

    • +25% Military Casualties
    • −25% Combat Gains
    • Destroys 3.5% of troops at home instantly upon arrival, and every 6 ticks 1.5% of troops at home are lost and current Building and Specialist Credits are reduced by 40%

    Ruby Dragon

    • −12.5% Military Effectiveness
    • +20% Military Wages
    • Destroys 3.5% of offensive and defensive specialists upon arrival, and every 6 ticks 2.5% of troops at home desert

    Topaz Dragon

    • −25% Building Efficiency
    • −25% Income
    • Destroys 10% of buildings instantly upon arrival, and every 6 ticks thereafter destroys 10% of buildings and 20% of current gold

    Sapphire Dragon

    • −35% WPA and TPA
    • −1 Mana Recovery
    • −1 Stealth Recovery
    • +12.5% Instant Spell and Sabotage Damage taken; −12.5% Instant and Sabotage Damage dealt
    • Destroys 30% of runes instantly upon arrival, and every 3 ticks thereafter destroys 25% of current rune supplies

    Personalities

    Note

    The Paladin, available in Age 115, was not available in Age 116. The Cleric returns after being absent since Age 113. The Sage is new to Age 116.

    The Artisan

    Bonuses

    • +25% Building Capacity (Homes, Stables, Dungeons)
    • +25% Building Production (Banks, Farms, Stables, Towers, Homes)
    • −25% Construction Costs (Including Raze)
    • Immune to Greed, Incite Riots, and Fool's Gold
    • +25% Economy Science Efficiency
    • Access to Ghost Workers, Greater Protection

    Starting Bonuses: +600 Soldiers, +600 Specialist Credits, +200 Building Credits

    Unique Passive: Master Craftsmanship — When razing buildings, Artisans recover 25% of buildings razed as Building Credits.

    The Cleric

    Bonuses

    • +1 Elite Defensive Value (affects NW)
    • +1 Defensive Specialist Strength (affects NW)
    • −40% Damage from Instant Spells
    • +25% Military Science Efficiency
    • Access to Salvation, Revelation, Divine Shield, Illuminate Shadows, Hero's Inspiration

    Starting Bonuses: +800 Soldiers, +800 Specialist Credits

    Unique Passive: Divine Favour — Self-spells cast by Clerics have their duration increased by 4 ticks.

    The General

    Bonuses

    • +1 General
    • +2 Offensive Elite Strength (affects NW)
    • −25% Training Cost & Speed
    • Can train one Elite for every two Specialist Credits
    • +25% Military Science Efficiency
    • Access to Mist, Wrath

    Starting Bonuses: +800 Soldiers, +800 Specialist Credits

    Unique Passive: Relentless Assault — Each successful attack against an enemy grants +5% Enemy Military Defensive Casualties against that enemy for 3 ticks, stacking up to +15%. Each new successful attack refreshes the duration of all stacks. If no successful attack is made for 3 ticks, the bonus resets. Attacking a different enemy also resets this effect.
